Apply for Registration


This is for Institutions who are interested to use the National Registration Bureau's KYC Portal to verify their customers' IDs. Note that the portal is currently only available for use by financial institutions, mainly banks, Insurance companies, Insurance blockers and Insurance agent organisations.

To apply, proceed to the next page, go through a 2-step application filling in forms with your institutions information, and the representative's identifications.

  1. A scanned copy of the Institution's certificate of registration
  2. A scanned copy of the license of authorization for the service provided
